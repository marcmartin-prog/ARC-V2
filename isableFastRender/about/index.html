ABOUT US

OUR MISSION


CONTACT US

ARC (Anabaptist Refugee Committee) is an Anabaptist organization focused on helping refugees from around the world. Although our main focus is on refugees who enter the United States through resettlement programs, we continue to seek new ways of reaching out.

OUR HISTORY

ARC Ministry was born out of a burden for the Afghan people being housed on the Fort McCoy Military base (Pictured) in WI. Men from various Anabaptist Churches witnessed firsthand the need for a Christian presence among these hurting people.
