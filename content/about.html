OUR MISSION


CONTACT US

ARC (Anabaptist Refugee Committee) is an Anabaptist organization focused on helping refugees from around the world. Although our main focus is on refugees who enter the United States through resettlement programs, we continue to seek new ways of reaching out.

OUR HISTORY

Current ARC operations are located in the city of Minneapolis. Here we interact with the local refugee population through English classes, shopping trips, and other cultural assimilation programs.
