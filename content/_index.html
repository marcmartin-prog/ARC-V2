ARC (Anabaptist Refugee Committee) is an Anabaptist organization focused on helping refugees from around thee world. Our main focus is to aid refugees entering United States through resettlement programs. We endeavor to be the hands and feet of Jesus through English classes, a sewing center, and assisting churches interested in joining the work.

CURRENT PROJECTS

Current ARC operations are located in the city of Minneapolis. Here we interact with the local refugee population through English classes, shopping trips, and other cultural assimilation programs.
